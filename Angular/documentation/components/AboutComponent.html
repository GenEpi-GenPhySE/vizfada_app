<h2>About FAANG</h2>

<p>Functionnal Annotation of the Animal Genome (<a href="https://www.faang.org/">FAANG</a>)
is an open consortia, notably gathering transcriptomic and epigenomic data in farm
animal species.
FAANG data is available on the FAANG data portal:
<a href="https://data.faang.org/">data.faang.org</a>.
</p>

<h2>Visualisations of FAANG data</h2>

<p>This web applications offers visualisations of FAANG data. Sample-sample correlation heatmaps
allow the exploration of the various FAANG datasets. Epigenetic stacks allow the
visual inspection of epigenetic profiles (ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq) at peaks, to visualy
investigate the strenghts of the signals, or at gene promoters, to investigate
the associations between epigenetic mark and gene expression levels.
</p>
